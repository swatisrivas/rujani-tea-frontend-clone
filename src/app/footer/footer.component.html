ABOUT THE SHOP

The story of Rujani Tea, started with a dream and a passion to bring the wonders of loose-leaf Assam tea to tea lovers across the world. We are fourth generation tea planters and have been producing award winning teas since 1897.

Assam tea is perceived to be just "tea in a bag". We hope to change that perception by bringing you a range of speciality teas, from our tea farm to your cup.
